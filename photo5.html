Volcanic Lightning at Mount Sakurajima

Photographer: Martin Rietze

Where Taken: Kyushu, Japan When: 2013

What It Shows: A dramatic moment where volcanic ash, lava bursts, and lightning flashes combine during an explosive eruption. The photo captures the raw force of nature — fire, ash, and electricity in a single frame. A rare, almost other-worldly natural phenomenon.
