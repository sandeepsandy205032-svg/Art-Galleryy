Earthrise

Photographer: William A. (Bill) Anders (Apollo 8 astronaut).

When: December 24, 1968 (Apollo 8 lunar orbit; the color shot is time-stamped during the mission).

Where it was taken: From lunar orbit aboard the Apollo 8 spacecraft — the Earth rising above the Moon’s horizon.

About the picture: This image shows Earth appearing to “rise” over the Moon’s barren surface as seen by the Apollo 8 crew — the first humans to orbit the Moon. It was taken with a modified Hasselblad 500 EL camera loaded with 70 mm Ektachrome film and quickly became one of the most influential environmental photographs ever, credited with helping change how people think about Earth as a single fragile planet.
